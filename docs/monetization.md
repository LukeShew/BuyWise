# Future Monetization

BuyWise can grow beyond a free price-checking MVP with a few buyer-focused revenue streams:

- Affiliate links for verified accessories, replacement parts, insurance, repair kits, and new-product alternatives.
- Premium price alerts for users watching specific products, price ranges, and local markets.
- Seller verification that checks receipt, serial number, ownership history, and seller reputation signals.
- Paid product reports for high-value purchases such as cameras, laptops, e-bikes, and pro monitors.
- Promoted marketplace listings that meet quality checks and are clearly labeled.
- Subscription for power buyers who flip gear, buy for teams, or monitor many categories.

The safest first paid feature is premium alerts because it does not weaken buyer trust. Promoted listings should only come after strong verification rules are in place.

Best positioning:

> Paste a used listing. BuyWise tells you if it is a deal, a trap, or overpriced.

The product should differentiate around the buyer decision, not just market value. Price guides already exist. BuyWise should focus on listing-specific risk, seller proof, negotiation, and category-specific checks.
